Batman's Diary: Shadow of Gotham

In the dark alleys of Gotham, the Dark Knight stands as a beacon against crime and corruption. But behind the mask lies a man with his own fears, hopes, and challenges. "Batman's Diary" offers a rare glimpse into the inner world of this enigmatic hero. Dive into his personal chronicles, from nightly patrols to confrontations with iconic foes, and discover the heart and soul of Gotham's silent guardian.
